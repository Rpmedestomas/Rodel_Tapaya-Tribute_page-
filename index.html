At the heart of Rodel Tapaya's work is his ongoing amalgamation of folk narrative and contemporary reality within the framework of memory and history.

Inherent in the work is a tension between objective investigations of art and the subjectivity of perception and experience, providing his work with an enigma that comes from the impossibility to paint a story without revealing inflections made by the painter's hand.
